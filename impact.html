Our Impact Vision

If Indian manufacturers and clusters adopt energy intelligence platforms at scale, the benefits extend far beyond cost savings. They reshape competitiveness, sustainability, and resilience.

What scaling energy intelligence could mean

Cost Stability

Reduced exposure to volatile energy markets through forecasting, hedging, and optimized procurement.

Carbon Reductions

Integrating renewables and tracking emissions in real time supports India’s climate commitments and CBAM readiness.

Export Competitiveness

Meeting ESG and carbon disclosure standards ensures continued market access and trust with global buyers.

Resilience

Clusters gain transparency across suppliers and processes, building resilience against shocks and supply disruptions.

Innovation & Jobs

Energy intelligence creates demand for skilled roles in data, sustainability, and advanced energy management.

Our role in this journey

Cleteco India is an early-stage company and building in public. We aim to prove this vision sector by sector, cluster by cluster, starting with textiles, steel, cement, chemicals, automotive, and FMCG.
