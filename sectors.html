Sectors we serve

We work with Indian manufacturers and clusters where energy cost, volatility, and export compliance matter the most. Each sector has different load shapes, process constraints, and reporting needs— The platform adapts.

Steel

Pain points

  • High, volatile power costs across EAFs, rolling mills, auxiliaries
  • Export-side CBAM/ESG disclosures for buyers
  • Complexity in open-access renewable integration

Quick wins

  • Shift scheduling with day‑ahead/TOU signals
  • Portfolio mix: grid vs OA RE vs contracts
  • Automated carbon intensity & buyer‑grade reports

Cement

Pain points

  • Thermal + electrical optimization across line operations
  • Demand spikes, reactive power, and penalties
  • Documentation for sustainability-linked finance

Quick wins

  • Peak‑shaving with dispatch guidance
  • Reactive power/contract demand monitoring
  • Consolidated emissions ledger & audit trails

Chemicals

Pain points

  • Continuous processes with safety & quality constraints
  • Fuel switching and captive vs grid trade‑offs
  • Supplier footprint and scope 3 visibility

Quick wins

  • Energy KPI tracking by line/unit
  • Scenario tests for fuel/contract options
  • Supplier data workflows for scope 3

Textiles

Pain points

  • Highly variable demand (dyeing/finishing cycles)
  • Export buyers requiring ESG evidence
  • Fragmented metering and bill data

Quick wins

  • Load scheduling with tariff/forecast signals
  • Buyer‑ready sustainability statements
  • Meter/ERP ingestion to a single dashboard

Automotive & Components

Pain points

  • Multiple facilities, suppliers, and logistics nodes
  • Just‑in‑time operations sensitive to price spikes
  • Scope 1–3 tracking for OEM programs

Quick wins

  • Multi‑site portfolio optimization
  • Alerts for tariff changes & anomalies
  • Supplier emissions data workflows

FMCG / Food & Beverage

Pain points

  • Cold chain & process loads with strict SLAs
  • Frequent starts/stops driving peak demand
  • Retail buyer ESG requirements

Quick wins

  • Peak demand management & alerts
  • Process‑aware schedule recommendations
  • Automated reporting for retail buyers
