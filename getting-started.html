Getting started

A lightweight path to value: quick discovery → focused data review → 30‑day pilot → scale with confidence.

Your 5‑step path

1) Discovery (15 min)

Understand your sites, loads, constraints, export requirements, and goals.

2) Data Review (2–3 hrs)

12 months’ bills, metering/SCADA (if available), contracts, and production cycles.

3) Business Case (1 week)

Baseline, scenarios, and a pilot plan aligned to cost, risk, and CBAM/ESG needs.

4) Pilot (30 days)

Limited rollout with KPIs—forecasts, alerts, optimization recommendations.

5) Scale (3–6 months)

Multi‑site deployment, open‑access renewables, training, reporting, and support.

What to prepare

Data

  • 12 months of electricity bills (PDF/CSV)
  • Meter/SCADA exports (if available)
  • Any PPAs / contract terms
  • Basic production schedule or shifts

People

  • Operations / maintenance contact
  • Procurement / finance contact
  • Sustainability/compliance contact (if any)
